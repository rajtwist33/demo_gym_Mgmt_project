 Note for Cron job implemention in FreeStyle GYm

1) DailyRunforpaymenttypecron => Set cron job for every day night at 1 pm.
2) Group Discount =>set cronjob for every month on some date.
3) Offer Discount =>set cronjob for every month on some date.
4) Anual Payment =>set cronjob for every month on some date.
